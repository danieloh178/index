Playstation 5

The PlayStation 5 is one of the latest tech from the Sony Interactive Entertainment line up. Announced in the year 2019 and offical released on November 12, 2020, the PlayStation 5 has sold over 10 million copies as of July 18, 2021

Specs

  • Custom 8-core AMD Zen 2
  • 16 GB GDDR6 SDRAM
  • Custom 825 GB SSD
  • Custom AMD RDNA 2
  • Ultra HD Blu Ray
